Staff Dashboard

Server Rules

  • Respect all members equally.
  • No discrimination, hate speech, or harassment.
  • Do not abuse your staff privileges.
  • Keep channels organized and topic-specific.
  • No spamming or advertising.
  • Follow server announcements and updates.
  • Always report serious issues to higher staff.
  • Protect sensitive information and member privacy.

Staff Roles & Responsibilities

  • Owner: Full control, manage all staff and server settings.
  • Sub Owner: Support Owner, manage high-level roles and configs.
  • Executive: Oversee staff performance and large server projects.
  • Manager: Moderate channels, assign tasks, enforce rules.
  • Dev: Maintain bots, scripts, and technical server operations.
  • Moderator: Monitor chats, warn or mute members when needed.
  • Helper: Assist members, answer questions, report problems.

Useful Commands

!ban, !kick, !warn, !mute, !clear, !verify, !info

Communication Guidelines

  • Always communicate politely with staff and members.
  • Use appropriate channels for discussions and announcements.
  • Escalate unresolved issues to Manager or Owner.
  • Document important events or incidents.

Training & Improvement

  • Review server rules periodically.
  • Train new staff members when needed.
  • Provide feedback to help improve team performance.
